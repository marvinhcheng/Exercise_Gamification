Exercise Tips

Working out is important but making sure you're working out often will help increase your progress towards your goals.
It is also important to remember to ensure you select a level of weights that you can safely and reliably do.
These are just a few exercises to give you something to think about. Try and find an exercise that is here or elsewhere
that you enjoy doing the most.




Upper Body Exercises

Bench Press: Focused on the pectoral muscles

Bicep Curl: Work the bicep muscles

Shoulder Press: Work out various muscles of the shoulder

Rows: Work the latissimus dorsi (lats) muscles

Core Exercises

Crunches: Work out the abdominal muscles

Lower Body Exercises

Squats: Work various leg muscles such as the glutes, quads, and hamstrings

Leg Press: An alternate way to work similar muscles mentioned above

Calf Raises: Works the calf muscles
