Exercise Tips - Calf Raises

Synopsis

Calf raises are important to improve the gastrocnemeus and soleus muscles, which are used for
walking, running, and balancing on a daily basis.

The Basics

Calf raises can be done either with a machine or on any surface that allows for a full range of motion.
For this exercise, it is important to stretch before you workout and also to not go too fast.
  1. Stand straight with your feet about shoulder width apart
  2. Raise your heels up so that you're balanced on your toes and front half of your feet
  3. Slowly lower your heels back down flat, and even at a negative angle if possible
  4. Repeat this for about 10-30 reps

Variations

Because it is a relatively simple exercise, there aren't many variations on this exercise. If you want to add
free weight dumbbells, hold it above your knees.
