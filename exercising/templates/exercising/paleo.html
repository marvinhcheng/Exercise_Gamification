Diet Tips - Paleo

Synopsis

The paleo diet is one that centers around foods that our paleolithic era ancestors would have eaten. This is where
the diet derives its name from. In doing so, you will need to cut out many foods that we don't realize have become
staples in our diet as a result of farming. This group includes foods that are grains, dairy, sugary, or generally
highly processed. Therefore, the diet consists of foods that are perceived to be more "natural" such as fruits, certain
vegetables, nuts, lean meats, or fish. It differs from other diets by cutting out grains and legumes, which are grown agriculturally.

Results

The paleo diet may be a good way to lose weight by limiting your intake of processed foods or calorie-dense foods.
However, the paleo diet hasn't received significant long-term studies into its efficacy and so its effects haven't been proven.
