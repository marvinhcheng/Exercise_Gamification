Diet Tips - Mediterranean

Synopsis

The mediterranean diet is one that focuses on the diets found in countries that surrounding the Mediterranean Sea.
There was some investigation into the diet they had, as researchers observed that these peoples were less likely to die from
coronary heart disease. The diet itself comprises mostly of vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and healthy fats, with occasional
fish, poultry, and dairy consumption. Notably, it limits the consumption of red meats such as beef.

Its simplicity

The diet is quite flexibile and is not necessarily like the more strict ones that primarily serve to help you lose weight. This
diet is simply one that presents healthy and tasty foods in a simple package. If it's a food you could imagine the Greeks traditionally
eating then you can have it as you like.
