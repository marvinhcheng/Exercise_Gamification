Diet Tips - Ketogenic

Synopsis

The ketogenic diet, or keto diet, when abbreviated, is a high fat diet that offers can offer health benefits.
Studies have shown that this type of diet can help you lose weight and improve your health.
Ketogenic diets may even provide benefits against certain disease such as diabetes, cancer, epilepsy, and Alzheimerâ€™s disease.

The Basics

The keto diet is a very low carb, high fat diet that focuses on replacing the first macronutrient with the second. This
causes a certain process in the body called ketosis, where fat becomes the primary source of energy.
Ketogenic diets are believed to reduce blood sugar and insulin levels. This and the increased amount of ketones can provide some health benefits. However, there is a complication called ketoacidosis that can come about from
too many ketones in the blood, causing it to become acidic.

Types of Keto Diets:

There are several versions, some of which are listed below:
  • Ketogenic diet (SKD): This version is a very low carb, moderate protein and high fat diet. It generally comprises of 70% fat, 20% protein, and only 10% carbs.

  • Cyclical ketogenic diet (CKD): This variation of the diet involves carb refeeding days, where you can have some more carbs and then go back to the keto diet.

  • Targeted ketogenic diet (TKD): This variation allows for carbohydrates around when you work out.

  • High protein ketogenic diet: This version of the keto diet is more protein-oriented, where protein comprises 35% of the diet, fat 60%, and carbs 5%.

What is Ketosis?

Ketosis is a metabolic state where your body switches to using fat as a primary source and not carbs. By reducing
reduing your carb intake, your body is not able to readily use glucose as an energy source, and thus breaks fat down
into ketones as an alternate source of energy. Important to know for ketosis is that protein can be turned into glucose
so it is important to limit protein to prevent that. Interestingly, ketosis can be measured by various tests and certain
symptoms like dry mouth or increased thirst.
